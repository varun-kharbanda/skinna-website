Frequently asked questions

Tap any question to expand the answer.

How does the AI actually work?

You take a selfie in good light, and SKINNA sends that single photo securely to our AI for analysis. The AI looks at five areas — skin health, hair, grooming, style fit, and overall presence — and turns what it sees into your SKINNA Score and a short, prioritised action plan.

It's designed to be encouraging and practical: every result points you toward a clear next step, never a judgement.

Is my photo stored anywhere?

No. Your selfie is analysed in real time and is not stored on our servers afterwards. Only the results of the analysis — your scores and recommendations — are saved to your account so you can track progress over time.

We never use your photos to train AI models, and we never share them with advertisers. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are billed through your app store, so you cancel there:

iPhone / iPad: Settings → tap your name → Subscriptions → SKINNA → Cancel Subscription.

Android: Open the Google Play Store → tap your profile icon → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → SKINNA → Cancel.

You'll keep access to paid features until the end of your current billing period.

How do I delete my account and data?

In the app, go to Profile → Privacy → "Delete all my data." This permanently removes your account, scan history, and profile information. It's immediate and can't be undone.

If you can't access the app for any reason, email us at support@skinna.xyz and we'll take care of it for you.

Is SKINNA safe for teens?

SKINNA is built for ages 16 and up. Everything about the experience is designed to be positive and forward-looking — we use gentle, motivational language, never harsh "ratings," and we build in daily limits to encourage a healthy relationship with the app rather than constant re-checking.

SKINNA is a wellness and self-care companion, not a measure of worth. If you ever feel distressed about your appearance, we encourage speaking with a trusted adult or a qualified professional.

Why did my score change between scans?

Lighting, camera angle, and expression all affect what the AI sees, so small movements between scans are normal. For the most consistent results, scan in soft, even daylight, hold the phone at eye level, and keep a relaxed, neutral expression.

Your score is best read as a trend over time, not a single number to obsess over.

Is SKINNA free?

Yes — you can start for free, with a set number of scans and core features included. Optional paid plans unlock more frequent scans and expanded guidance. You'll always see the price clearly before you commit, and there's no charge to download or create an account.

Does SKINNA give medical or dermatological advice?

No. SKINNA offers general self-care, grooming, and style suggestions for informational purposes — it is not a medical device and does not diagnose or treat any condition. For anything health-related, including skin concerns, please consult a qualified dermatologist or doctor. See our Terms of Service for the full disclaimer.
