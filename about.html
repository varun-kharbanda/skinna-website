Our story

Built to lift you up —
not tear you down.

SKINNA started with a simple belief: technology that looks at your face should make you feel more confident, not less.

Most apps in this space were built to judge. They hand out harsh "ratings," compare you to impossible standards, and leave you feeling smaller than when you opened them. We wanted to build the opposite.

Why SKINNA exists

Looking after yourself shouldn't feel like an exam. Yet for years, the only tools that analysed how you look were cold, clinical, and quietly cruel — designed to rank people rather than support them.

SKINNA flips that on its head. Take one selfie and you get your personal SKINNA Score across five everyday areas — skin health, hair, grooming, style fit, and presence — followed by a short, doable plan ranked by what will help you most. It's the difference between being told "here's what's wrong with you" and "here's your next small win."

Every score is framed around your potential. Every tip points forward. And we deliberately build in gentle limits, because confidence comes from showing up for yourself consistently — not from refreshing a number a hundred times a day.

What we stand for

🤝

For everyone

Every age from 16 up, every gender, every skin tone. SKINNA adapts its guidance to you — not to a single narrow ideal.

🔒

Privacy first

Your selfies are analysed in real time and never stored on our servers. Your face is yours, full stop.

🌱

Wellbeing over vanity

We measure progress, not perfection — with positive language and healthy limits baked in by design.

Where we're going

SKINNA is India-first and built for the world. We're starting with the core loop — scan, score, plan, repeat — and growing it into a genuine confidence companion that grows alongside you. We'd love for you to be part of these early days.
